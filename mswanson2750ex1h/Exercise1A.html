Description:

Streaming Website: A streaming website that will keep track of two parties primarily-viewers and streamers. The website will keep track of user information such as followers and substriptions(both who an individual is following and and who is followed by that individual). Additionally, it will also keep track of veiws on vods, clips, etc.

Use Cases:

As a user, I want to follow streamers so that I can get notifications

As a streamer, I want to see views for vods, clips and total channels views

As a user/streamer, I want to create sharable clips

As a streamer, I want people to have the option to subscribe to be for added benefits

As a streamer, I want subs to be giftable

As a viewer, I want to browse streams by category

As a streamer, I want to add emotes availible to subs

As a streamer, I want to promote users to moderaters(access to basic commands/ban users)
